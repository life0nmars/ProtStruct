Firstly, we will remind you the composition of a nucleotide.

It contains of three parts:

  • Nucleobase
    • This part determines your nucleotide, it can be purine or pyrimidine. Interestingly, purin means pure urine , because it was exctracted from urine, pyrimidin name comes from its exctraction method, the synthesis, performed in high temperatures ('pyr' - fire).
  • Five carbon sugar molecule
    • Desoxyribose stands for DNA and Ribose - for RNA. Together nucleobase with sugar forms nucleoside (from glycoside bond)
  • Phosphate group
    • The phosphate group is bonded to 5' atom of the sugar and can be included in mono-, di- and triphosphate forms. Nomenclature names, therefore, would be nucleosid mono/di/triphosphate
